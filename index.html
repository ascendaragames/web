LOST AT SEA

Step into the role of a solitary lighthouse keeper stationed on a remote coast, where your duty is simple but unforgiving: keep the light burning and survive the sea’s relentless rhythm.

Lighthouse Keeper: Lost at Sea is an atmospheric single-player survival game focused on routine, responsibility, and isolation. Each day revolves around maintaining the lighthouse, managing limited supplies, staying warm and fed, and observing the ocean for passing ships and changing weather. Calm, meditative tasks are constantly tested by storms, mechanical failures, and unexpected emergencies that demand quick decisions.

Venture out by boat to nearby islands to gather resources, uncover quiet story moments, and face the risks of open water. After violent weather, return to a damaged lighthouse that must be repaired as soon as possible. Use binoculars to scan the horizon, track incoming storms, and search for distant signals in the fog.

With a minimal interface and a grounded, realistic setting, the game emphasizes immersion, environmental storytelling, and survival against nature itself. Time, weather, and the steady pressure of isolation are the only forces working against you.

Keep the light. Alone.
